Job Description

We’re looking for a passionate Web Developer to lead the development and ongoing maintenance of our website. You’ll be responsible for creating a fast, functional, and visually appealing website that reflects our brand and enhances the customer experience. From updating menus to launching new promotions, your work will directly impact how customers interact with Crazy Cow online.

Responsabilities

  • Develop, manage, and maintain the company website
  • Ensure responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
  • Implement new features and updates (e.g. promotions, seasonal menus, locations)
  • Optimize site speed, SEO, and user experience
  • Monitor website performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Collaborate with marketing and design teams to align the website with brand goals
  • Set up and manage basic analytics and tracking tools

    • Minimun Requirements

  • Proven experience in front-end and back-end web development
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and at least one backend language (e.g. PHP, Python, or Node.js)
  • Experience with CMS platforms (such as WordPress or similar)
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g. Git)
  • Basic understanding of SEO principles
  • Good eye for design and user experience
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines

    • Nice to have

  • Experience with e-commerce integrations or online ordering systems
  • Basic graphic design skills
  • Knowledge of performance optimization and accessibility standards
